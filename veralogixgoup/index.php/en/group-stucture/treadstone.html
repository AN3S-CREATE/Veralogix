Overview

Treadstone

Treadstone specializes in the transportation of coal that has reached export quality. The company efficiently moves coal to allocated sidings for transfer by rail to ports, ensuring timely delivery and facilitating international trade.

Market Focus

Treadstone operates within the coal logistics market, focusing on efficiently transporting coal products to meet growing demand in international markets.

Business Development

Treadstone is dedicated to expanding its logistics network and exploring new partnerships with rail and port authorities to enhance its service efficiency and market competitiveness.
