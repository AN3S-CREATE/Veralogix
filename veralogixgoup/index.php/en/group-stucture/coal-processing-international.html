Overview

Coal Processing International

Coal Processing International provides skilled personnel to operate and maintain wash plant facilities. Their expertise ensures efficient coal processing, maintains high standards and contributes to the overall productivity of mining operations.

Market Focus

CPI operates within the coal processing sector, supporting mining companies that require skilled labour to optimize their washing and processing facilities.

Business Development

CPI is focused on expanding its service offerings by training and developing personnel to meet evolving industry standards, enhancing its competitive edge in the coal processing market.
