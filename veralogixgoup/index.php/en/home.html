About Us

Joining The Dots
in the Coal Value Chain

Welcome to Veralogix, where our primary focus is to approach the coal market from a non-traditional service standpoint through our unique Reverse Value Curve. This curve is focused on not selling locally but creating a closed-circuit route to the export market.

In the past seven years, our businesss unique Selling Point has proven valuable. It distinguished Veralogix as a pioneering coal trader with a footprint that extends its value curve throughout mining, processing, transportation, and distribution, allowing it to expand rapidly.
