Our Market

  • You are here:

  • Our Market

Since inception the vision of being a mining company with a unique offering has materialised and the strategy of reverse integration has given Veralogix a key advantage over many of its competitors in the traditional trading space.

Not only do we have a route to market strategy but have successfully developed and integrated a “Pit to Port” solution into our business model for our customers.

This business model has not only allowed Veralogix to profit throughout the whole value chain but has also given us the ability to withstand market downturns and grow while other traditional traders were hampered by prevailing market conditions.

This strategy proved its strength during the continuation of the COVID-19 pandemic, during which many traders struggled, but Veralogix’s business grew by almost 250%.

Social Upliftment. Local Empowerment.

Schools Rugby Teams

As a response corporate citizen, Veralogix is committed to uplifting our communities through the promotion of sports and a dedication to helping alleviate 121,312 social ills like drug and alcohol abuse. By promoting and investing in youth sports, we aim to make a substantial social impact in our area of operation and the broader Mpumalanga community.

Orphanages

Through our social partner MAM, Veralogix Group makes regular donations to orphanages, victim support centres and rehabilitation centres in our community. In 2024 our Easter donation drive saw us donating more than 4500 warm meals to communities in and around Middelburg. This initiative brought a magical moment of hope to the thousands of those who are less fortunate than us including the Middelburg Care Village, Zuid Street Children's Home, and the GBV victim support centre.

Blanket Drives

In 2024 our Mandela Month initiative saw us donating more than 500 blankets and 1500 sanitary towels to disadvantaged communities, particularly those in the farm areas surrounding our operations. We donated 150 blankets to the elderly and infirm in Mhluzi We donated 150 blankets to a church in Hendrina and another 50 blankets to a care village in Kwa-Zamokuhle We donated 150 blankets to the farming communities in Woestalleen

Borehole Drilling

Veralogix has committed to drilling no less than 8 boreholes for the surrounding farm communities within and around our area of operation. Having launched the initiative during Mandela Month and together with our partner company's we aim to deliver clean drinking water, even for the most far flung communities. In partnership with and through MAM, communities can submit their requests on and ongoing basis in order to benefit from our community initiatives
